Your Safety is Our Priority

All treatments are performed by a licensed Nurse Practitioner with extensive training in facial anatomy, injection techniques, and emergency procedures.

Contraindications

• Pregnancy or breastfeeding

• Active skin infections at treatment site

• Known allergies to treatment components

• Certain neurological conditions

• Blood clotting disorders

Possible Side Effects

• Temporary redness or swelling

• Minor bruising at injection sites

• Temporary headache

• Asymmetry (rare, correctable)

• Allergic reactions (very rare)

Emergency Protocols

• Immediate access to emergency supplies

• Trained in vascular occlusion management

• Hospital partnerships for severe reactions

• 24/7 emergency contact available

• Hyaluronidase on-site for reversals
